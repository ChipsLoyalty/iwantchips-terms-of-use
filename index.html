1. WHO WE ARE AND WHAT WE DO.

IOU Corp. (“IOU Corp,” "we," "us," or "our") provides a mobile and web-based application that allows users to earn points and track rewards for purchases made from participating vendors (“App”). The App provides an online marketplace for distributors to offer rewards for purchases, and for customers to search and find valid rewards that they qualify for. Through the App, customers and distributors can arrange to make transactions, whereby the customer will go to the distributor’s facility to complete the transaction and pick up any rewards being redeemed. The App provides reward-redemption tracking and product performance data based upon such transactions (all of the above features are the “Services”).

11. REWARDS.

Upon completing a transaction facilitated through the App, purchasers may scan the QR code provided by the distributor within the App to accrue points (referred to as “chips” inside of the App). Any points consumers accrue will then be stored in a digital wallet within the App. Consumer users may spend points after they’ve accrued them by then producing redemption barcodes from within the App that can be presented at a participating distributor’s location in exchange for rewards. You agree to abide by all applicable laws and these Terms of Use when earning and redeeming awards. IOU Corp’ records will be deemed the official records for rewards. IOU Corp cannot guarantee points will accrue to your account if IOU Corp cannot track the applicable QR codes accurately.
